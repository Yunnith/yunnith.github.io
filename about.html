About me

Hello! My real name is Judith, but you can call me Yunnith :)
I'm a graphic designer specialiced in UX/UI design. I also love drawing, enjoy programming, and have an unyielding desire to keep learning no matter the field: languages, digital illustration, Gestalt, 3D printing, and so on. I'm a very creative person who likes to work in a structured way and my mantra when working is "Trust the process!".

My way to approach design always includes observation, research, exploration, defining structure, intent, and refining details.
Illustration plays an important role in my creative process. It allows me to experiment freely with color, form, and personality, and to approach ideas in a more intuitive and playful way. This sense of play often feeds back into my design work, helping me create experiences that feel more human, expressive, and engaging, even when working within structured systems.

Alongside my work as a graphic designer, I’ve gained experience in roles related to communication, coordination, and content creation. Working in different environments has helped me develop adaptability, empathy, and a strong sense of responsibility, which strongly influence how I approach design and collaboration today.
